Joining my group

If you are interested in joining my group as a postdoc, PhD, or visiting student, welcome! I am really happy that you would consider joining us in Denmark! You can either apply for an open position, or apply for your own funding. All open positions calls will be advertized at the ITU vacancies page. If you would consider getting your own funding, the following instruments may be of interest for you.

Before you get in touch with me about any of the opportunities, please make sure to,

  • Get in touch early (even before the call has opened). It will take you more than a month to write a good application.
  • Decide on a couple of papers of mine that you have read in detail, find interesting, and could see yourself contributing to further exploration of.
  • Have one or two project ideas that are reasonably developed.

When you contact me, write a bit about yourself, your project idea, and why you think I would be a good match as your mentor. Finally, do attach a CV to your email. Thanks for your interest, and I look forward to hearing from you!
